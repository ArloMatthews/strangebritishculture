INTERESTING FOOD CHOICES

While Biritish cuisine is a very respected part of their own culture, a lot of their dishes have just been turned into staple foods by other countries. For example, while baked beans might be more of a classic British food, those who live in other countries who have beans as sort of a foundational food might think more plainly of it. Same goes for a lot of dishes based around sausage or potatoes (looking at you “bangers and mash”). "If the British can survive their food, they can surive anything," once said Nobel Prize-winning Irish playwrite George Bernard Shaw. Below are some of the foods that are simply strange, unsual, or downright unappetizing.

Spotted Dick: a type of sponge pudding made with suet and an unfortuante name

Stargazy Pie: a pie featuring potatoes, eggs, and visible fish heads for display

Kippers: smoked fish for breakfast. Need I say more?

Brown Sauce: malt vinegar, tomatoes, dates, and other spices make this exist somehow

Black Pudding: a type of sausage made out of animal scraps

White Pudding: like black pudding, minus the blood

Toad in the Hole: a bunch of sausages are thrown into a batter

Haggis: a sheep's heart, liver, and lungs with oatmeal and onions

Offal: Basically just prepped up animal organs. Basically the same wod as awful so...

Jellied Eels: Essentially just an eel paste. Please do not put this on a sandwich

Laverbread: An edible boiled and puréed seaweed dish

Pease Pudding: Peas are boiled and puréed and made into a "pudding"
